History

Founded by Mrs. Smith in 1971, Durham House of Pets has been the leading distributor of animal apparel and foods items in New Hampshire. Orginally just selling dog treats in the greater Durham area, a demand for the treats lead to the expansion of the business.

Why Use Us?

Here at Durham House of Pets we take pride in the quality and health benefits of our food products as well as the uniqueness of our apparel. Everything at Durham House of Pets is naturally grown and comes from the United States.
